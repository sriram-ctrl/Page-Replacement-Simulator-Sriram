FIFO (First-In First-Out): Replaces the page that has been in memory the longest. The oldest page is always the victim, regardless of how frequently or recently it was used.
LRU (Least Recently Used): Replaces the page that has not been used for the longest period of time. Tracks the recency of page accesses.
Optimal (Bélády's Algorithm): Replaces the page that will not be used for the longest time in the future. Theoretical benchmark — requires future knowledge.
